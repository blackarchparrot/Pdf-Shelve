📤 Contribute a PDF

Have useful books, notes, or questions? Share the shareable link with everyone!

❓ Need help getting your link? ▼

For Google Drive:

  1. Right-click your PDF file (or tap the 3 dots on mobile) and select Share > Share.
  2. Under General access, change Restricted to Anyone with the link.
  3. Ensure the role on the right is set to Viewer.
  4. Click Copy link and paste it into the field above!

💡 Using OneDrive / Mega / DropBox? Just ensure link access is set to "Public" or "Anyone with link can view" before copying.
